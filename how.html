How it works

STEP 1: SELECT

Scroll through all your memories and select the one to capture! Tell us about it and easy-peazy step 1 completes.

STEP 2: KNOCK KNOCK

Open the door and get ready to cast your memory into a frame! Cherish Moment's team will come to your place with all the equipments and get the cast impression of your hand.

STEP 3: CAST

Now, that you invited us, let's capture the impression to be made. Just dip your hand and ta-da!

STEP 4: FRAME

Great! You decided the memory and you got it casted, now what frame you want for the casted memory to be in? We got you covered, don't worry. We'll send you the options.

STEP 5: RECIEVE

Great journey with us, indeed. Within 3 days we get the memory captured into a frame. Do tell us what you want to capture?
