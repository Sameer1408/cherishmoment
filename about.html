About Us

Welcome to Cherish Moment

The best thing about memories is creating them ......

In 90s or early 2000s, we used to make albums of all the memories of our childhood, of our grandparents and the birthdays. There was always a special photographer for the same. However, you ever considered the moment is gone and unless you open that album, the memory is fading away.

Cherish moment understands the value of a memory. Even a few seconds of the first walk a child takes becomes the happiest memory of a parent. The grandparents blessings and the moment they become friends with you is perhaps your best memory of them. All the memories are stored in the locker of our brain. To capture such emotions, we cast your memories into a beautiful frame.

We all respect art and are artists in Cherish Moment. We saw the moment passing by and no way to capture it in our hands. The moment is gone but the memory remains. Cherish moment gives you the way to remember the moment forever.

Let's celebrate the memories!
